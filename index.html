Who We Are

Bridging Ancient
Wisdom with
Modern Nutrition

TMJ Foods was born from a simple conviction — Africa's indigenous grains hold the answer to the modern health crisis. We process sorghum, unripe plantain, orange-fleshed sweet potato, and tapioca into clean, functional foods that support gut health, blood sugar balance, and everyday vitality.

We work closely with smallholder farmers across Nigeria — paying fair prices, ensuring traceability, and keeping wealth within our communities.
